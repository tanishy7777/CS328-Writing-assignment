# Final Writeup Template

## Title

Economy, Energy Mix, and Responsibility in Global CO2 Emissions

## Introduction

Use 100-150 words. Introduce the OWID CO2 dataset, the research question, and the limitation that the analysis is observational.

## Person 1: Economy

Use 250-300 words.

Graphs to include:
- GDP per capita vs CO2 per capita.
- GDP per capita vs CO2 per GDP, or a small outlier table.

Conclusion to aim for:
GDP per capita is strongly associated with CO2 per capita, but GDP alone does not explain carbon intensity.

## Person 2: Energy Mix

Use 250-300 words.

Graphs to include:
- Coal share vs CO2 per unit energy.
- Energy per capita vs CO2 per capita.

Conclusion to aim for:
Energy use explains much of the emissions story, but fuel mix determines how dirty that energy use becomes.

## Person 3: Policy, Trade, and Structural Change

Use 250-300 words.

Graphs to include:
- Territorial vs consumption-based CO2.
- Indexed CO2 emissions trend since 1990 or 2005.

Conclusion to aim for:
Responsibility changes under consumption accounting, and some countries show real declines over time, though this dataset alone cannot prove which policy caused them.

## Final Conclusion

Use 100-150 words.

Bring the three themes together:
CO2 emissions depend on economic development, energy structure, and accounting method. A country can look high-emitting because it is populous, energy-intensive, coal-heavy, export-oriented, import-dependent, or historically industrialized.
